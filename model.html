MOTHER TERESA

ABOUT:

  • Born: August 26, 1910, Skopje, Macedonia (FYROM)
  • Died: September 5, 1997, Kolkata
  • Full name: Blessed Mother Teresa of Calcutta
  • Education: Loreto Abbey, Rathfarnham (1928–1929)
  • Awards: Nobel Peace Prize, Bharat Ratna, Templeton Prize, more
